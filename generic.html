Airbnb New Users Destination Prediction

For this case, we build a recommendation model from user profile and session data to predict a new user's first booking destination after performing extensive feature engineering and comparing different models such as Bernoulli Naives Bayes and XGBoost. The output would allow the Airbnb to offer personlized experience and better forecast demand. The project was done based on Airbnb recruitment competition hosted on kaggle.

April 15, 2022

FairStorage - Demand Forecasting of Store Sales

A classic demand forecast of the sales for the next 21 days using historical sales data of the past 1920 days. This solution illustrates the advantage of leveraging LGBM algorithm and feature engineering to better represent the target variable and why performing data analysis to understand the layout of the data and choosing an appropriate metric is important for tuning your model. We also provide suggestions on how to improve the efficiency of the model using refined data collection methods and taking the help of SMEs.

The Road Not Taken
Road Quality Monitoring

In this project, we build an efficient object detection model using YOLOv5 to classify and estimate the severity of defects in Indian Roads that would help in replacing a highly inefficient and manual process of road quality assessment in India. The model could be integrated with a camera mounted on a car to perform monthly checks of the roads within city limits, upload the geo-tagged data for batch prediction, and update the quality level in different areas on a dashboard.

May 15, 2021

Vehicle Re-Identification Using Computer Vision

Vehicle re-identification (re-id) aims to solve the problems of matching and identifying the same vehicle under the scenes across different surveillance cameras. Use cases include the identification of adversarial vehicles where its extremely important to locate the target vehicle quickly and accurately. In this project, we employ a siamese-model coupled with negative samples and triplet loss to re-id the vehicle. We also identify the attributes of the vehicle such as type, color etc.. to be used as features.
